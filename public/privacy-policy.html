Privacy Policy for PicPlate

Effective Date: April 7, 2025

Last Updated: April 7, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to PicPlate – AI-Powered Mood-Based Recipe & Restaurant Recommender. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you access and use our Single Page Application (SPA). Please read this policy carefully. By using our application, you agree to the terms described herein.

Note: PicPlate is an independent service and is not affiliated with or endorsed by Pinterest. When you connect your Pinterest account, your data is used solely for the functionality provided by this app and is handled in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

2. Data We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Authentication Data: When you log in via Google or email, we collect basic account information (e.g., name, email address) to create and manage your user account.
  • Pinterest Data: If you choose to connect your Pinterest account, we retrieve images solely for analysis. We do not store your Pinterest credentials.
  • Uploaded Images: Images that you select and upload from Pinterest are processed in real time for image analysis. These images are not stored permanently.

2.2 Data Processed by APIs

  • Google Vision API: Analyzes uploaded images to detect faces, emotions, and dominant colors. Faces are automatically blurred to protect privacy.
  • Google Gemini API: Uses the processed image data (emotion/color analysis) and your location to suggest mood-based healthy recipes and nearby healthy restaurants, including generating food images.

2.3 Usage Data

  • User Preferences and Logs: We store your search logs, analysis results, Gemini recommendations, and user preferences in Google Firestore to enhance your experience and provide personalized suggestions.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • Image Analysis: To analyze uploaded images for mood and color information, enabling us to offer healthy recipe and restaurant recommendations.
  • Personalization: To tailor suggestions based on your uploaded content, location, and user preferences.
  • Service Improvement: To understand usage patterns and improve app functionality, ensuring a seamless and secure user experience.
  • Communication: To provide support, send important updates, and respond to your inquiries.

4. Third-Party Services

We rely on trusted third-party services to operate our application:

  • Google Vision API: Processes images to detect faces, emotions, and colors. Note that detected faces are blurred for your privacy.
  • Google Gemini API: Generates recipe suggestions and restaurant recommendations based on processed image data and user location.
  • Google Firestore: Securely stores user logs and preferences.

These third-party services have their own privacy policies which you are encouraged to review.

5. Data Storage and Security

  • Data Storage: User data (such as search logs and recommendations) is stored in Google Firestore. Uploaded images are processed and then not retained beyond what is necessary for immediate analysis.
  • Data Security: We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. Our infrastructure is regularly monitored and updated to ensure data integrity and confidentiality.
  • Data Retention: We retain user data only as long as necessary for the purposes described in this policy. You may request deletion of your data at any time as outlined in Section 7.

6. Sharing and Disclosure of Information

  • Third-Party Disclosure: We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal data to third parties except as necessary to provide our service or as required by law.
  • Pinterest Data: Images retrieved from Pinterest are used exclusively for in-app analysis. We do not share your Pinterest data with any unauthorized third parties.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose information if required by law or in response to a valid request from public authorities.

7. Your Rights and Choices

  • Access and Correction: You have the right to access and correct any personal data we hold about you.
  • Data Deletion: You may request that we delete your data at any time. Upon request, we will promptly remove your personal information from our databases.
  • Opt-Out: You may disconnect your Pinterest account or opt-out of certain data collection practices via your account settings.
  • Contact Us: For any questions, requests, or concerns about your data, please contact us at abhate@horizon.csueastbay.edu.
